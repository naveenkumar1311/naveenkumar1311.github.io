About Me

Hi, I am K Naveen Kumar, currently a PhD Research Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, India. My area of research includes scene perception and path planning in Autonomous Vehicle Technology. Previously an MTech Research Assistant at Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, Telangana, India. I have done my BTech from the Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara, Gujarat India.

I am fueled by my passion in the areas of machine learning/deep learning, cloud computing to solve real-world problems. I am also an Android and PHP developer with experience as both frontend and backend developer in a deep learning project. I also completed a couple of online courses in Coursera. Some of my work experiences include websites of an IIT Hyderbad Professor Dr C Krishna Mohan and Assistant professor Dr Digvijay Singh Pawar [my name can be seen at the end of the website]. My interest in research has led me to submit a conference paper to IEEE International Conference on Cloud Computing for Emerging Markets 2018 (acceptance rate is less than 10% for conference papers) for a work which is done as part of my 3rd-year B.Tech summer project. The paper has been accepted and won the best paper award in 2018.

I am also interested in Deployable AI in making final deployable solutions in solving problems. I along with a team of 4 stood as top 10 finalists in Nvidia AI Hackathon 2019 where we worked on the topic Audio alert system for visually impaired using pixel segmented data. I have also been selected in the IITH-Ritsumeikan University Japan Project-based Learning program under Sakura Science Exchange 2019 wherein I got an opportunity to visit Japan and work with Japanese students.

Currently, I am working in various domains like Medical and Health Science, Autonomous Vehicle Technology, Action recognition, Video Surveillance, Smart Human Mobility all using Deep learning.

I am practicing and doing realistic portrait art for 2 years now which led me to start a commission work-based business called Sketch-IFY. I aspire to excel in the area of academic research along with becoming an Entrepreneur. We can discuss more on Linkedin

Sketch-IFY (Sketch I For You to Amplify your Smile) - An artist networking platform to save Indian endangered artforms

Description : Our idea fits into a category of service innovation because we are developing a process and a systematic framework that will use technology to leverage and promote artforms to a dynamic business model.
Mission: The mission of Sketch-IFY is to provide an efficient platform that will be inclusive of areas that are related to art in one or the other way. The platform will serve as a teaching aid for beginners who wish to be an artist, a digital art gallery with global reach for artists to get their art valued, the platform will act as a morale booster for those who are giving up on our native art styles due to lack of exposure and monitory justification of their artifacts. We want to rejuvenate arts which are about to get extinct, for example Mithila painting, Dhokhra handicraft, Roghan painting, etc (https://www.indiatimes.com/culture/who-we-are/9-indian-art-forms-that-could-disappear-forever-if-they-re-not-saved-250533.html). We want our platform to: Support art, To save endangered artforms, Connect art needs with artists, Provide a global art gallery, To connect art related businesses together, Bring smiles
To summarise, Sketch-IFY is all about bringing smile on faces of those who wants art in their life in every possible way; be it an artist, an art lover, art gallery owner or small scale business man whose business either depends on arts or can assist to step up art.
